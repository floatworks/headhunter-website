Candidates

As specialist automotive recruitment consultants, working in a specific technical market ensures that we are in regular contact with the relevant employers in the market place and can provide a high level of service, market knowledge and automotive jobs that other generalist agencies cannot.

Following initial contact with you, we will consider your motivation for moving and previous experience against out current automotive jobs. Using our market knowledge we will then draw up a list of suitable companies to approach on your behalf that may be interested in your experience and be able to offer you the automotive job you are looking for.
