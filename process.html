Process

In order to ensure the fast and efficient process we promise, we will need to quickly understand why someone would want to leave their current employers and join your organisation. In order to achieve that we request you complete an online questionnaire which will help us know more about you whilst identifying the key characteristics of profile you are looking for.

From here our consultants will begin the research stage and gather intelligence data. With our extensive knowledge of the commercial environment we have the ability to understand organisational hierarchies, giving our consultants the ability to map talents within a business.

At this stage our consultants will have a series of telephone conversations and where appropriate meetings to gauge the person.s interest whilst allowing the consultant to covertly interview them for suitability.

Once suitability and interest is established, the candidate will undergo a more vigorous interview process, to include psychometric testing where deemed appropriate. All our agents are familiar with NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming), giving the consultant multiple tools to screen the individual and ensure the correct person is selected for the role.

Finally we would present our candidates to you for them to undergo your interview process. We will act as the interface between yourselves and the candidates and will manage expectations from both sides.

Our average assignment is 6 weeks but clearly this can vary, although for a properly executed search we would say that 6 weeks represents a sensible time frame.
