About

We are specialist executive headhunters with a low touch approach. We work with the top automotive companies in the United Kingdom to deliver only the highest level talent. Our understanding of the markets, the businesses and individuals within these markets enables us to facilitate the perfect match between all parties.

Low touch

We strive to only deliver candidates to companies only when asked to. We do not have an office full of recruiters harassing companies or clients for work. With a small team we can focus on specific needs of our clients and keep our retainer lower.
